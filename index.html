Baghdad Gardening Guide

Discover plants that thrive in Baghdad's climate

Climate: Hot Arid (BWh)

Gardening Tips for Baghdad

Watering

Water early morning or late evening to reduce evaporation. Drip irrigation is ideal.

Sun Protection

Use shade cloth for delicate plants during peak summer months (June-August).

Planting Seasons

Best planting times are February-April (spring) and September-October (fall).
